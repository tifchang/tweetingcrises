Professor Tambe's Most Frequently Used Words

A breakdown by class, driven primarily by curriculum.


Industries are dynamically shifting, and the significance data plays in terms of decision making continues to grow. Professor Tambe is a passionate advocate for equipping students at Wharton with the necessary tools to enter all industries. He believes in fully grasping the fundamental data tools, like R, tableau, and S.Q.L. (he says 'es-kew-el', not sequel, he's a man of his time), to develop upon future skills like "machine learning", and throw around big words with integrity like "big data". How did I jump to this conclusion? By listening to him in lecture.

If you listen to his word choice, you might just understand what this man is all about.

This analysis focuses on the word choice Professor Tambe uses over the course of OIDD 215. It provides unique insights into the course's primary focus as well as probably the most important 10 things you should take away from this class.
